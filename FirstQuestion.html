The Amrita School of Computing, a premier constituent of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence and research-driven innovation.

With campuses across Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Coimbatore, Chennai, Kochi, and Mysuru, the school offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, including specialized tracks in Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, and Data Science.
Accredited with an A++ grade by NAAC, the institution blends technical rigor with a "Compassion-Driven Research" philosophy, encouraging students to develop technological solutions for societal challenges such as healthcare, sustainability, and disaster management. The school is well-regarded for its robust industry connections and impressive placement record, consistently attracting top-tier global recruiters like Microsoft, Amazon, and Cisco. In recent cycles, students have secured high-value packages, with some reaching as high as 80.4 LPA at the Coimbatore campus.
Beyond academics, the School of Computing fosters a vibrant ecosystem through international collaborations, specialized research labs, and active student chapters like the ACM.
This holistic approach ensures that graduates are not only technically proficient in modern stacks like Python, Java, and C++, but also equipped with the ethical foundation and leadership skills required to excel in the global tech landscape.
